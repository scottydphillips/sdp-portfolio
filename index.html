Scott Phillips is a former professional musician and aspiring web developer. He is from the Phoenix metro area and currently resides in Marysville, MI. He is currently studying full stack design and engineering through Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp. Prior to learning web design, Scott has performed in a number of notable places, with some renowned groups and was Assistant Professor of Music at Abraham Baldwin Agricultural College in Tifton, GA. He attended Northern Arizona University and Florida State University.
